We have made contacting our support team easier than ever! 

There are multiple ways to get in touch with us.  You can search our help center for an article to answer your questions.  This helps our customers locate any relevant help articles prior to submitting a ticket request.  This article will go over where you can submit tickets and [view and track](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/211014457-How-do-I-submit-a-support-ticket-#h_01G8GH735SKP2GV4BDZMRCV51E) the tickets you have submitted. 

Here are all of the ways you can get in contact with us: 

-   [Our Help Center](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/211014457-How-do-I-submit-a-support-ticket-#h_01EQ8PRV2CJ129VSS89QR91MN9)
-   [In DrChrono](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/211014457-How-do-I-submit-a-support-ticket-#h_01EQ8PS4DRVVEYBN6GZVN1MWNJ)
-   [On the iPad EHR](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/211014457-How-do-I-submit-a-support-ticket-#h_01EQ8PSBSK1HYV1VXXQX86VDT4)

#### Contacting Support through the Support Webpage

1\. Please visit: [drchrono.com/help](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) and fill out the pertinent information. 

[![Submit_a_Request_.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/4873311030299)](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/211014457-How-do-I-submit-a-support-ticket-#h_01EQ8PRV2CJ129VSS89QR91MN9)

2\. As you begin typing into the Subject field, our smart search will appear and suggest articles that match what you are searching for as seen here. 

![Submit a Support Ticket Example.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19703150076699)

3\. Now you can either access an article to answer your question or if no articles match what you're looking for, simply click Submit.  If need be, you also have the option to attach a file to provide a visual of what is going on.

![Submit_Request_.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/4873363930395)

### Contacting  Support inside of DrChrono (Web) 

There are a couple of ways to submit a ticket on the web.

1\. Go to Help > Support Center. 

![Help_Support_center_Full_Screen.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/4871574292891)

2\. Now you'll be redirected to our [Support Page](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us).  Click Submit a request and follow the steps [above](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/211014457-How-do-I-submit-a-support-ticket-#h_01EQ8PRV2CJ129VSS89QR91MN9). 

![Help_Center_Submit_a_Request_Enlarged.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/4871892849947)

--OR--

1\. Click on the Support button in the lower right.  From here, you can search for a topic or start a [Live Chat](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042649592) session. 

![Chat Support .png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19703107774491)

#### Contacting Support via the iPad EHR app

1\. Tap on Account in the bottom right corner of the screen. 

![EHR_Page_Account.jpeg](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/4873456007579)

2\. Once there, tap on Help on the left side. 

![Account_Help_Button_.PNG](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19483200788251)

3\. You can search our Help Center or tap the (![Support_Ticket_Icon_Ipad.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19483200800539)) icon to submit a ticket. 

![Help_Center_Ipad.PNG](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19483192161819)

4\. Press Start a Conversation. 

![Start_a_Conversation.PNG](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19483192170907)

5\. Enter your information and press Send.  A support agent will review your request and will reach out to assist.

![Contact_Us_Support_.PNG](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/19483192172699)

### Managing Your Tickets

After you have submitted your ticket, you can view your tickets on the help center.  

1\. Click Sign In to log in to the help center.

![Help_Center_Sign_In_.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/7499906965019)

2\. Click on your name, and select My Activities.

![Help_Center_My_Activities_.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/7500008842267)

3\. Here you will be able to see a list of your tickets and the status of those tickets in a dashboard format. This will help you organize your list of active tickets. 

![Help_Center_Ticket_Dashboard_Edited.png](https://support.drchrono.com/hc/article_attachments/7500136416283)
